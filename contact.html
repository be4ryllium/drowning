Where to Contact Help

If someone is in immediate danger of drowning, the best and safest choice will always be calling 911.

To prevent drowning from happening in the future, there are many more options, both local and national.

  • One option is to visit the Red Cross for the Imperial Valley at
    312 South 8th St. El Centro, CA 92243
  • Another option is to call the National Drowning Prevention Alliance at
    1-800-757-9270
