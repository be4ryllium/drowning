Facts About Drowning

  • Drowning is the leading cause of death in children ages 1-4.
  • Drowning is the second leading cause of death for children ages 5-14.
  • Every year in America, there are an estimated 4000 fatal drowning incidents, and 8000 nonfatal drowning incidents.
  • Every day, around 10 people drown and an estimated 2 of those will be people under 14.
  • For every child who dies from drowning, another five receive emergency care for injuries received from drowning.
